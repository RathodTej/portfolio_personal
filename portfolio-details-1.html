AR-Expo

Project information

  • Project Name: AR-Expo
  • Project date: September 2023
  • Category: Augmented Reality (AR)
  • Tools: Unity 3D, C #, Vuforia-Image Tracking, Sketchfab
  • Project URL: https://youtu.be/aw6NEv2IsHE

Embark on a captivating journey with AR_Expo, my latest project that harnesses the transformative capabilities of Vuforia's augmented reality technology. This innovative application invites users to step into a virtual exhibition space, where a diverse array of 3D models is brought to life through marker-based tracking. The precision of Vuforia ensures a seamless and accurate augmentation, allowing users to explore and interact with detailed models ranging from awe-inspiring architectural wonders to intricate machinery. Immerse yourself in the experience as you dynamically witness captivating animations. Beyond the visual spectacle, AR_Expo adds an educational dimension by providing insightful information about each showcased creation. Users can delve into the history, functionality, and design aspects, making the augmented reality journey both engaging and informative. Join me in pushing the boundaries of augmented reality, where the virtual and real worlds converge to create an immersive and enlightening experience like never before.
