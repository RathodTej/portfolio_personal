VR home tour

Project information

  • Project Name: VR home tour
  • Project date: August 2022
  • Category: virtual Reality (VR)
  • Tools: Unity 3D, C #, Assets Store, Sketchfab
  • Project URL: https://youtu.be/LMCljv5tbDs

"VR Home Tour," our innovative project developed at TechXR during the course period, invites users to step into the future of virtual reality exploration. Leveraging ray-casting technology on mobile devices, we have crafted an immersive experience that allows users to trigger position changes and open doors with a mere gaze, enhancing the sense of presence and engagement. This project not only showcases the seamless integration of VR into the mobile realm but also underscores the potential for interactive and intuitive experiences that redefine how we interact with digital environments. Immerse yourself in meticulously designed virtual spaces, where every detail, from architecture to decor, comes to life. Our VR Home Tour transcends traditional boundaries, offering a glimpse into the possibilities of immersive mobile VR experiences. Through the collaboration and support provided by TechXR, we have pushed the limits of technology and design, creating a project that paves the way for the future of virtual exploration on handheld devices.
