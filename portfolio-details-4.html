AR Car Garage

Project information

  • Project Name: AR Car Garage
  • Project date: july 2022
  • Category: Augmented Reality (AR)
  • Tools: Unity 3D, C #, Vuforia-Image Tracking, Depth Mask, Sketchfab
  • Project URL: https://youtube.com/shorts/5OdhaI4SZeU?feature=share

In my "AR Car Garage" project, I delved into the world of augmented reality, crafting an immersive experience using Vuforia's cutting-edge image tracking technology. This application transforms the mundane concept of a car garage into a captivating digital realm. Through the lens of a device, users can explore a virtual garage that seamlessly integrates with the real world, thanks to the precision of Vuforia's image tracking. What sets this project apart is the implementation of a depth mask effect, adding a layer of realism to the virtual environment. To further enhance user engagement, dynamic animations bring the virtual garage to life, creating an interactive and responsive experience. The goal was not just to showcase a garage but to redefine the entire perception of it, blending the physical and digital seamlessly. This project represents a fusion of innovation and technology, providing users with a unique and unforgettable encounter with a car garage through the lens of augmented reality.
