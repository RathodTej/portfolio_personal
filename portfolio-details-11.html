VR Interior Design

Project information

  • Project Name: VR Interior Design
  • Project date: February 2023
  • Category: virtual Reality (VR)
  • Tools: Unity 3D, C #, Assets Store,TechXR Controller, Sketchfab
  • Project URL: https://youtube.com/shorts/MQzOP7yVImk?feature=share

"Experience the future of interior design with 'VR Interior Design,' a revolutionary project developed at TechXR during our course period. Utilizing the TechXR controller on mobile devices, users can virtually explore and customize their dream homes in three dimensions. This innovative project not only showcases the seamless integration of VR technology but also envisions a collaborative platform for homeowners, architects, and designers to make real-time decisions in a shared virtual space, pushing the boundaries of traditional design processes.

At its core, 'VR Interior Design' transcends static blueprints, offering an intuitive and dynamic experience where users can experiment with color schemes, and architectural elements. This project represents a significant leap into the future of home planning, where the intersection of technology and design opens up endless possibilities. Welcome to a new era of immersive and collaborative interior design that empowers users to shape their living spaces in unprecedented ways."
