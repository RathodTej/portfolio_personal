AR Diwali Wishes

Project information

  • Project Name: AR Diwali Wishes
  • Project date: November 2023
  • Category: Augmented Reality (AR)
  • Tools: Unity 3D, C #, AR Foundation-Ground Tracking, Sketchfab,Blender
  • Project URL: https://youtube.com/shorts/CYFNdt1ZwXo?feature=share

In my "AR Diwali Wishes" project, I delved into the realm of cutting-edge technology by harnessing the power of augmented reality (AR) through the AR Foundation framework. This innovative application transforms the Diwali celebration experience, offering users a captivating blend of traditional festivities and modern technology. With a strong emphasis on ground tracking, the AR Diwali Wishes app allows users to seamlessly integrate virtual Diwali greetings into their physical surroundings, ensuring a stable and realistic placement of augmented elements. The incorporation of dynamic animations adds an extra layer of enchantment, bringing the Diwali wishes to life with visual flair and interactivity. By fusing AR Foundation's capabilities, ground tracking, and animated elements, the project not only showcases my technical proficiency in AR development but also provides users with a personalized and immersive Diwali celebration, where the boundaries between the virtual and real worlds blur to create a truly memorable experience.
