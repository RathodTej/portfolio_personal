VR jet engine

Project information

  • Project Name: VR jet engine
  • Project date: February 2023
  • Category: virtual Reality (VR)
  • Tools: Unity 3D, C #, Assets Store,TechXR Controller, Sketchfab
  • Project URL: https://youtube.com/shorts/pw_Z2bwFxyY?feature=share

"VR Jet Engine, a project developed during my course at TechXR, brings the fascinating world of aerospace technology to life through immersive virtual reality (VR) experiences on mobile devices. Utilizing the advanced TechXR controller, users can delve into the intricate details of a jet engine, interactively manipulating and exploring its components. This educational endeavor not only enhances understanding of complex engineering concepts but also showcases the potential of mobile VR platforms for delivering engaging, hands-on learning experiences.

With the TechXR controller, users can seamlessly disassemble and reassemble parts, fostering spatial awareness and dexterity in a captivating VR environment. VR Jet Engine is a pioneering educational tool that bridges the gap between theory and practice, making the exploration of aerospace technology both accessible and exhilarating."
