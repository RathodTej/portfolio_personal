AR Gun Store

Project information

  • Project Name: AR Gun Store
  • Project date: August 2022
  • Category: Augmented Reality (AR)
  • Tools: Unity 3D, C #, Vuforia-Multi Tracking, Sketchfab
  • Project URL: https://youtube.com/shorts/mntWjY9FCMw?feature=share

"The AR Gun Store project marked an exciting exploration into the realm of augmented reality (AR) by harnessing the power of Vuforia. With a primary focus on delivering an immersive user experience, the project aimed to revolutionize how individuals interact with firearms virtually.

Leveraging Vuforia's multi-tracking capabilities, users could seamlessly integrate lifelike 3D models of various guns into their real-world environment, creating a visually compelling and interactive scenario. The integration of animations further elevated the project, injecting dynamic elements into the virtual experience. Users not only had the opportunity to observe and analyze different types of guns in a real-world context but also witnessed captivating animations that added an extra layer of engagement.

The AR Gun Store project successfully merged cutting-edge AR technology with Vuforia's robust features."
