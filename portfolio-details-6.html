AR Bike Expo

Project information

  • Project Name: AR Bike Expo
  • Project date: June 2023
  • Category: Augmented Reality (AR)
  • Tools: Unity 3D, C #, AR Foundation-Image Tracking, Sketchfab
  • Project URL: https://youtube.com/shorts/U6MwfqJe5Mk?feature=share

The "AR Bike Expo" is a cutting-edge augmented reality project that I spearheaded, utilizing AR Foundation technology to bring an exhilarating experience to users. Through the seamless integration of AR Foundation, the project ensures a smooth cross-platform functionality on Android devices. The highlight of this application lies in its use of image tracking technology, enabling users to unlock the augmented reality experience by pointing their device's camera at specific images. Once activated, a virtual sports bike materializes in the real world, allowing users to explore every detail through their device. This immersive showcase offers a dynamic and interactive encounter with sports bikes, providing enthusiasts with a unique opportunity to appreciate the intricate designs and features, all enhanced by captivating animations. The project not only demonstrates technical prowess but also caters to the passion and curiosity of bike aficionados, offering a thrilling fusion of technology and automotive enthusiasm.
